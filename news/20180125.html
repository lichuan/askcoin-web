Askcoin PRE-SALE

2018-01-25 Source: Askcoin

At present, askcoin is in the pre-sale stage, through the Ethereum smart contract system.
The contract address is 0xc00F7746c5C3cE7616d0aA35607658E547306Dc0
And the ratio is: 1ETH = 500000ASK
As long as you send ETH to the contract address, it will automatically release the corresponding ASK ERC20 tokens to you, the contract is open source code, you can refer to the following url:
https://etherscan.io/address/0xc00F7746c5C3cE7616d0aA35607658E547306Dc0#code
When the mainnet of Askcoin is launched in the future, you can call the “setMainnetAccount” function of the contract to register your Askcoin’s account, so that we can send the real Askcoin to you.
